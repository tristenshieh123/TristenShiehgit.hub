Culture

In my culture we celebrate Chinese new year. During Chinese new year we have many events to celebrate the new year.

These events are often dancing dragons, lion dancing, or family events. In my culture, red signifies good fortune, luck and prosperity.

As Children, many people receive these red envelopes that include money called HongBao at Lunar Year.

This symbolises good wishes and luck for the new year ahead.
